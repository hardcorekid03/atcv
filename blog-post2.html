Keep a Positive Attitude at Work

Posted 06 March 2023

In any workplace, it is common for attitudes and behaviors to change over time. As we interact with our colleagues and encounter new challenges, our perspectives can shift, and our professional relationships can evolve. Facing changing attitudes of workmates can be challenging, but it also presents an opportunity to demonstrate a positive and professional attitude.

“Never forget what you are, the rest of the world will not. Wear it like armor and it can never be used to hurt you.” - Tyrion Lannister

When I first started my job, I quickly formed strong relationships with my workmates. However, as time went on, I noticed that some of my colleagues began to adopt a negative and unproductive attitude. They complained about their workload, criticized management decisions, and seemed to lack motivation and enthusiasm for their work.

At first, I found myself getting frustrated and discouraged by their negativity. However, I soon realized that I had a choice in how I responded to their attitude. I could either allow their negativity to bring me down, or I could take a positive and professional approach.

I chose the latter. I made a conscious effort to remain positive and enthusiastic about my work, even when my colleagues were not. I also made an effort to be supportive and encouraging towards my colleagues, even when I disagreed with their attitude.

Over time, I began to notice a shift in my colleagues' attitudes. They seemed to respond positively to my positivity and began to adopt a more productive and motivated attitude. By taking a positive and professional approach, I was able to influence the attitudes and behaviors of those around me, and create a more positive and productive work environment.

In conclusion, facing changing attitudes of workmates can be a challenge, but it also presents an opportunity to demonstrate a positive and professional attitude. By remaining positive and enthusiastic, even in the face of negativity, we can influence those around us and create a more productive and fulfilling work environment.
